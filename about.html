Frequently Asked Questions

Always here for you

Got questions? We've got answers.

How much does it cost to rent outdoor gear?
Our pricing starts from just $10/day for individual items like hiking poles or headlamps, up to $60/day for complete climbing bundles. Group kits start from $99. No hidden fees — what you see is what you pay.
What gear categories do you offer?
We offer 4 main categories: Camping (tents, sleeping bags, stoves), Hiking (backpacks, boots, poles), Climbing (harnesses, ropes, helmets), and Water Sports (kayaks, canoes, paddleboards). We also offer curated adventure bundles for groups.
Are there discounts for longer rentals or members?
Yes! Weekly rentals get 20% off, and our TrailGear Members Club members enjoy exclusive seasonal discounts, early access to new gear, and priority booking at peak times.
Can I extend my rental period mid-trip?
Absolutely. Call or message our 24/7 support team to extend your rental before your return date. Extensions are subject to availability and billed at your original daily rate.
What happens if gear gets damaged?
We offer an optional damage waiver for 15% of your rental total that covers accidental damage. Normal wear and tear is never charged. For gear loss or major damage without the waiver, replacement costs at 50% of retail apply.
